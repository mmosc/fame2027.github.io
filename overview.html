Challenge Overview

CHALLENGE OVERVIEW

Motivation

Multimodal speaker classification systems typically assume the availability of all modalities during inference. However, in real-world scenarios such as multimedia retrieval, surveillance, and teleconferencing, one or more modalities may be unavailable due to occlusion, sensor failure, or data corruption.

Additionally, most existing systems are trained and tested on the same language, limiting their applicability in multilingual environments.

This challenge aims to push the boundaries of:

  • Missing modality learning
  • Multimodal robustness
  • Cross-lingual speaker classification
